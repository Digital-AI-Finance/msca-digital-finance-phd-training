MSCA DIGITAL Finance PhD Training Programme

Marie Sklodowska-Curie Doctoral Network — Grant No. 101119635

The MSCA DIGITAL programme trains 17 doctoral candidates across 19 partner institutions in 11 countries, combining cutting-edge research in AI, blockchain, cybersecurity, and sustainable finance with intensive industry secondments. The structured training curriculum comprises 6 mandatory courses, 18 advanced electives, and 16 transferable-skills modules.
