### **Orientation Training Digital Finance**

- Leading institution: WU Vienna University of Economics and Business

- EC: 3


### General Description

This meeting enables doctoral fellows to meet European researchers in Digital Finance outside the doctoral network. The meeting will be preceded by a general training course in Digital Finance.
