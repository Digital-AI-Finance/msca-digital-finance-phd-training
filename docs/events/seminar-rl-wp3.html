Research Seminar: Reinforcement Learning Applications in Finance (WP3)

Time & Location

16 Apr 2025, 11:00 – 11:45

Microsoft Teams

About the event

The WP3 meeting and research seminar includes a research talk by Mathis Jander, who is working on Reinforcement Applications in Finance.

During the presentation, Mathis will share his insights into the existing literature on Reinforcement Learning in financial markets and discuss its epistemological and methodological shortcomings. He will also propose an alternative approach to advance the field.

Link to participate: Join the meeting now

Speaker

Mathis Jander is a doctoral candidate at the University of Twente within the MSCA Doctoral Network for Digital Finance. He holds a bachelor’s degree in industrial engineering and a master’s degree in data science. His Individual Research Project (IRP1) focuses on the applicability of Reinforcement Learning to Digital Finance.
