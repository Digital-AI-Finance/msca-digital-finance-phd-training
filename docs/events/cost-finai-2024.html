COST FinAI PhD School on Fintech and AI in Finance 2024

June 10 - 14, 2024

Enschede, the Netherlands

Summary

Welcome to the COST FinAI PhD School on Fintech and AI in Finance 2024!

We are excited to announce a further edition of this COST Action CA19130 Training series. This meeting is organized by the COST Action CA19130 on Fintech and AI in Finance, under the auspices of COST - European Cooperation in Science and Technology. The event will showcase the Action's expertise with a focus on  integrating advanced computational technologies with financial practices. Expert speakers will delve into the transformative potential of Generative AI in finance, discussing its role in automation and decision-making. Topics include the deployment of chatbots and large language models in financial technologies, highlighting innovations in customer interaction and financial advising.

Some facts about the COST Action 19130

\- 51 countries

\- 200+ researchers from 100+ universities

\- Funded by the Horizon Europe Framework Programme of the EU

Keywords

Fintech, Artificial Intelligence in Finance, Transparency in Finance, Machine Learning, Blockchain, Cryptocurrencies.

This event is based upon work from COST Action 19130, supported by COST (European Cooperation in Science and Technology), www.cost.eu.
