Research Seminar: Explainable AI Methods for Financial Time Series (WP3)

Time & Location

04 Jun 2025, 11:00 – 12:00

Microsoft Teams

About the event

The WP3 meeting and research seminar includes a research talk by Jens Reil, who is working on Explainable AI Methods for Financial Time Series.

Link to participate: Join the meeting now

Speaker

Jens Reil is a doctoral candidate at the Bern University of Applied Sciences within the MSCA Doctoral Network for Digital Finance. He holds a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering and two master's degrees in the areas of financial engineering & management, data science & business, and enterprise architecture & IT management. His Individual Research Project (IRP16) focuses on the utility of classical XAI methods in financial time series.
