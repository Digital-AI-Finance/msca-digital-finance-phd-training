Mid-term Review Event

  • Leading institution: The Babes-Bolyai University
  • EC: 1

General Description

The mid-term review event is a key event for the DIGITAL network that will bring together researchers, industry, regulators and supervisors. The training component of the meeting is a short course on “Sustainability in Digital Finance”.
