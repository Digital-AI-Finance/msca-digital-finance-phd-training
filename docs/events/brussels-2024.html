COST FinAI Meets Brussels

Policy Implications 2024

May 14 - 15, 2024

Brussels, Belgium

Summary

Welcome to the COST FinAI Brussels Meets!

We are excited to announce our 2nd meeting of the COST Action CA19130 Meets Brussels series. This meeting is organized by the COST Action CA19130 on Fintech and AI in Finance, under the auspices of COST - European Cooperation in Science and Technology. The event will showcase the Action's findings with a focus on policy implications. Representatives from working groups will report on their activities, and EU policymakers and industrial partners will share their perspectives and engage in a dialogue with participants of the Action.

Some facts about the COST Action 19130

\- 51 countries

\- 200+ researchers from 100+ universities

\- Funded by the Horizon Europe Framework Programme of the EU

Keywords

FinTech, Artificial Intelligence in Finance, Transparency in Finance, Machine Learning, Blockchain, Cryptocurrencies.

This event is based upon work from COST Action 19130, supported by COST (European Cooperation in Science and Technology), www.cost.eu.
